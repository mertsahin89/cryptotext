About Cryptotext

The ICP ecosystem is thriving and everyone is looking forward to the upcoming ICP-Bitcoin direct integration. We truly believe that ICP's direct integration with Ethereum and Bitcoin is going to be one of the most momentous development in the crypto ecosystem and 2022 will be an excellent year for IC.

As a group of crypto and ICP enthusiasts, we have created a really unique and vintage ASCII Art NFT collection for this huge development . Many of us performed this art in our own way when there was no Whatsapp or emojis and as children of the 90s, we are really excited about the preserving this nostalgic art and pass on to web3, which is the most interesting area of technology for us.

We created this collection by using the logos of the first 200 projects in the crypto ecosystem. To create our collection, we selected the top 200 ranking of projects available on coinmarketcap at the start of 2022 by total market cap and we chose January 3, the day Satoshi Nakamoto mined Bitcoin's genesis block. We did not collaborate with any artists during the production of this collection; instead, we created an ASCII art generator specifically for this concept and reorganized each logo to achieve the greatest results from the generator. We term what we produce "pure programmer art."

Each NFT consists of a pure ASCII art html file generated by our own coded generator and a fancy 3D animated version of this artwork which we formed with our own CSS code. These two files work interacted and smoothly with one another, and we believe they give a distinct and unique NFT experience when compared to NFTs consists of usual image files such as PNGs ,GIFs and SVGs etc. Furthermore, each logo produced by the ASCII art generator includes texts containing various key concepts of that project, expressions used by that project to describe themselves on their websites and whitepapers, abbreviations of various cryptographic techniques, and the names of popular applications in their ecosystems.

We aim for this collection to be a snapshot of the first 200 blockchain projects in the year ICP's Bitcoin and Ethereum integration took place and it gives us great pleasure to be able to host such an NFT on-chain thanks to the Internet Computer.

There are many good projects as well as many not so good projects in the crypto ecosystem, but regardless of the projects, we can say that most projects have great logos. While creating this series, we ignored our judgments about the projects and tried to define those projects in their own words with the texts inside each logo. Since each project has a unique logo, there is no rarity index in this collection and we are very curious how the ICP community will rate these awesome ASCII art logos.

Besides being the first ASCII art collectibles on Internet Computer , each cryptoText NFT will grant airdrop rights for our coding-themed play to earn NFT game, which we have already begun to develop and also all of our future projects.
